About TechLift Africa

TechLift Africa is a pan-African technology enablement platform focused on building mobile-first SaaS, delivering vocational & developer training, and partnering with governments and MNOs to launch scalable digital services. We structure pilots to measure social impact while building product-market fit for each local context.

Mission

Accelerate inclusive digital transformation across Africa.

Vision

A digitally empowered Africa where technology lifts livelihoods.

Quick Facts

  • HQ: Accra / Nairobi (recommended)
  • Focus: EdTech, FinTech, GovTech, HealthTech
  • Model: SaaS + Training + Implementation
  • Target: 52 countries, local offices
